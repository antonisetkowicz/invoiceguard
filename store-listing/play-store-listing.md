## Full Description (4000 chars max)
VoxDrop is the anonymous voice note app where your voice reaches one random stranger anywhere in the world.

No followers. No feed. No social graph. Just your voice, launched into the void.

HOW IT WORKS:

1. RECORD — Hold to record up to 30 seconds of audio. Say whatever's on your mind.

2. SEND — Your voice note gets delivered to one random person on the planet. You don't choose who. The universe does.

3. LISTEN — Open your inbox to hear voice notes from strangers around the world. You never know what you'll get.

4. REACT — Respond with a single emoji or up to 40 characters of text. That's it. Keep it brief.

5. REVEAL — Felt a connection? Tap "Reveal" to let the other person know. If they tap it too, you've matched. Pay a small fee to unlock each other's profiles and start a real conversation.

WHY VOXDROP?

In a world of curated profiles and filtered photos, VoxDrop strips it all away. No algorithms deciding what you see. No follower counts. No clout chasing. Just raw, unfiltered human voices connecting across the globe.

Every voice note is a tiny act of vulnerability — sending a piece of yourself to someone you'll never see. Unless you both want to.

FEATURES:
- Anonymous by default — no signup required to start
- Hold-to-record with live waveform visualizer
- 30-second max keeps it real and spontaneous
- Emoji or short text responses only
- Mutual reveal system — both sides must opt in
- Dark, minimal UI designed for late-night vibes

PRIVACY:
- Your voice notes are stored securely and delivered to only one person
- No voice notes are public — ever
- Profiles are only revealed after mutual consent AND payment
- We don't sell your data or track your social connections (you don't have any here)

Download VoxDrop. Press record. Say something real.
